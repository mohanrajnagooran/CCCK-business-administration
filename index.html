Online Course Bachelor's Degree In Business Administration

Earn your Bachelor's Degree In Business Administration through Central Christian College of Kansas. Our fully online program is designed for working professionals and adult learners seeking flexibility and academic excellence.

Program Overview

The Bachelor's Degree In Business Administration provides a strong foundation in Business Administration theory, Business Management, and Marketing and Sales. Graduates are prepared for careers in Entrepreneurship and Small Business, Further Education & Leadership, mental health support, education, and Finance and Accounting.
